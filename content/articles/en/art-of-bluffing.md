# The Art of Bluffing — Online Poker Strategy

A **bluff** is a tool, not a goal. It works in the right spot at the right size. Before bluffing at [Baatryn Öröö](https://pppoker.mn/?lang=en), nail [position](https://pppoker.mn/en/articles/table-position-poker/) and [starting hands](https://pppoker.mn/en/articles/starting-hand-selection/).

## When bluffs work

- From the button on dry boards
- Opponent checks often and looks weak
- Small pot, high fold equity
- Your image is tight

## When not to bluff

- Multiway large pots
- «Revenge» mindset — see [psychology](https://pppoker.mn/en/articles/poker-psychology-mindset/)
- Strong obvious lines from opponent
- Beyond bankroll comfort

## Sizing

- Small bluff: enough to deny equity
- Large bluff: only with a clear plan
- Never shove your whole stack on a random bluff
