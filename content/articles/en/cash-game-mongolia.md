## What are blinds?

Blinds = forced bets. At Baatryn Öröö:

- **250/500** — small bankroll, learning table
- **1K/2K — 10K/20K** — experienced players

Example: 10,000₮ at 250/500 = 20 big blinds — OK to learn, but manage bankroll.

## Buy-in

Minimum chips to sit. Usually 20–100 big blinds. Too short = high bust risk; too deep = too much on one table.

**Tip:** Beginners — low blinds, ~30–40 buy-in rule for Hold'em.
