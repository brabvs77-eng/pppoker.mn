# Hold'em vs Omaha — Pick Your Online Poker Format

**Hold'em vs Omaha** is the top format question for Mongolia players. [Baatryn Öröö](https://pppoker.mn/?lang=en) runs both: Hold'em 9-max, Omaha 6-max. Beginners should start with Hold'em.

## Quick comparison

| | Texas Hold'em | Omaha (PLO) |
|--|---------------|-------------|
| Hole cards | 2 | 4 |
| Hand rule | Any 5 cards | Must use 2 hole + 3 board |
| Action | Medium | High |
| Pots | Medium | Large |
| Beginners | Yes | After experience |

## Hold'em highlights

- Most popular **online poker** format worldwide
- 2 cards — simpler math
- Easier to learn position and [starting hands](https://pppoker.mn/en/articles/starting-hand-selection/)

Baatryn Öröö: blinds 250/500 — 10K/20K, cash 24/7.

## Omaha (PLO) — when to switch

- 4 cards — more quads and full houses
- Bigger pots — higher variance
- Try after you know [hand rankings](https://pppoker.mn/en/articles/poker-hand-rankings/)

Baatryn Öröö: 6-max, VIP tables available.

## Which fits you?

**Choose Hold'em** if you are:
- New to poker
- Learning steady strategy
- Starting from 10,000₮ bankroll

**Choose Omaha** if you:
- Have Hold'em experience
- Want more action and big pots
- Can handle swings
