# Statistics & Analysis — Improve Online Poker

**Poker statistics** show where you win and leak chips. At [Baatryn Öröö](https://pppoker.mn/?lang=en), beginners can start with simple notes — no HUD required.

## Why stats matter

Feelings lie: «I crush the button» might be true — or your BB might be bleeding. Numbers tell the story.

## Core stats (beginners)

| Stat | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| VPIP | % of hands you play |
| PFR | % you raise pre-flop |
| 3-bet | Re-raise frequency |
| WTSD | Went to showdown % |

Beginner targets (Hold'em 9-max): VPIP 15–22%, PFR 12–18%.

## Manual tracking (100 hands)

Log each session:

- Position (EP, MP, BTN, BB)
- Hand (AA, AK, 72o…)
- Result (+ / −)
- Note (tilt?)

Pair with [bankroll management](https://pppoker.mn/en/articles/bankroll-management/).

## Find leaks

- Playing too many hands? → [Starting hands](https://pppoker.mn/en/articles/starting-hand-selection/)
- Losing in BB? → [Table position](https://pppoker.mn/en/articles/table-position-poker/)
- Big pot losses? → [Psychology](https://pppoker.mn/en/articles/poker-psychology-mindset/)

## Next step

After 500+ logged hands, decide whether to change stakes or format. Compare [Hold'em vs Omaha](https://pppoker.mn/en/articles/holdem-vs-omaha/).
