# Deposit & Withdrawal — Online Poker Mongolia

**Deposit** and **withdrawal** are the main trust test when you pick an online poker club. [Baatryn Öröö](https://pppoker.mn/?lang=en) supports IBAN in tögrög and USDT (TRC20) for qualified players.

## Key limits

| | Amount / timing |
|--|-----------------|
| Min. deposit | 10,000₮ |
| Min. withdrawal | 10,000₮ |
| Deposit time | Usually 10 min – 2–4 hours |
| Withdrawal time | From ~10 min up to a few hours |

## How to deposit

1. Install the [PPPoker app](https://pppoker.mn/en/articles/mongol-poker-download/)
2. Join Baatryn Öröö club
3. Cashier → choose **IBAN**
4. Transfer per instructions
5. Double-check your IBAN number

## USDT (TRC20)

After **100,000₮+** total IBAN deposits, USDT becomes available — useful if you prefer crypto.

## How to withdraw

1. Cashier → withdrawal
2. Enter your IBAN account
3. Minimum: 10,000₮
4. Wait for confirmation

Issues? Contact [@BatrynOrooSupport](https://t.me/BatrynOrooSupport) immediately.

## Picking a reliable club

- Do withdrawals arrive on time?
- Is support 24/7?
- Are minimums clear?
