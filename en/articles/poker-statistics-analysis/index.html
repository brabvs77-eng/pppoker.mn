Statistics & Analysis — Improve Online Poker

Poker statistics show where you win and leak chips. At Baatryn Öröö, beginners can start with simple notes — no HUD required.

Why stats matter

Feelings lie: «I crush the button» might be true — or your BB might be bleeding. Numbers tell the story.

Core stats (beginners)

Stat Meaning
VPIP % of hands you play
PFR % you raise pre-flop
3-bet Re-raise frequency
WTSD Went to showdown %

Beginner targets (Hold'em 9-max): VPIP 15–22%, PFR 12–18%.

Manual tracking (100 hands)

Log each session:

  • Position (EP, MP, BTN, BB)
  • Hand (AA, AK, 72o…)
  • Result (+ / −)
  • Note (tilt?)

Pair with bankroll management.

Find leaks

  • Playing too many hands? → Starting hands
  • Losing in BB? → Table position
  • Big pot losses? → Psychology

Next step

After 500+ logged hands, decide whether to change stakes or format. Compare Hold'em vs Omaha.
