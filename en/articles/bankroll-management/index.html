Bankroll Management — Play Online Poker Longer

Bankroll management means planning how much poker money you have and how many buy-ins you risk per table. Baatryn Öröö lets you start from 10,000₮ — but sizing matters.

What is a buy-in?

Chips you bring to one table. Example: 20,000₮ at 250/500 = 40 big blinds.

Recommended depth

Format Bankroll (buy-ins)
Hold'em cash 30–40 buy-ins
Omaha cash 50–70 buy-ins

Omaha needs more because variance is higher.

Starting with 10,000₮

Minimum deposit is 10,000₮ — enough for ~20–40 BB at 250/500. Do not put your entire roll on one table.

Moving up stakes

Move up only when bankroll reaches ~30 buy-ins for the next level. Be ready to move back down after losses.

Mindset

After bad beats, don't chase — change tables or stop. Set session limits.
